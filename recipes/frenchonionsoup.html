Vegan French Onion Soup

A vegan french onion soup with carmalized onions, potatoes, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup Vegan Butter
  • 4 Large Sweet Onions
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 2 Dried Bay Leaves
  • 2 Fresh Thyme Sprigs
  • 1 Cup White Wine
  • 2 Large Russet Potatoes
  • 2 Quarts Vegetable Broth
  • 1 Cup Raw Cashews (soaked for at least 8 hours)
  • 1/4 Cupe Nutritional Yeast
  • 1 Tablespoon Lemon Juice
  • 2 Tablespoons Corn Starch
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Steps

  1. Heat the butter in a large pot. Add onions and saute. Stir constantly. Add salt and pepper. Saute until translucent, reduce heat as needed. About 5 minutes.
  2. Reduce heat to medium low and cook onions until fully carmalized. Takes about 30-45 minutes to caramelize.
  3. Add garlic, bay laeves, thyme, diced potatoes, and white wine. Simmer for about 10 minutes or until wine is mostly absorbed by onions and tomatoes.
  4. Make cashew cream. Drain cashwes and add to blender with 1 cup of the vegetable broth, nutritional yeast, lemon juice, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Blend until smooth.
  5. Add corn startch to pot, coat potatoes and onions.
  6. Add remaining 7 cups of vegetable broth and cashew cream. Stir to combine,
  7. Simmer on medium low, bubble for 20 minutes.
